About Us...

Energy for a World in Motion



Hi, we're Helios Energy


Helios is a forward-thinking solar energy solutions provider dedicated to transforming lives and businesses across Zambia and beyond. We specialize in delivering affordable, reliable, and sustainable solar energy systems to farmers, small businesses, and households. Our mission is to bridge the energy gap, drive economic growth, and foster environmental sustainability by making clean energy accessible to all.



Our Vision


We envision a future where every individual, business, and community has access to clean, renewable energy. By harnessing the power of the sun, we aim to create a self-sustaining energy ecosystem that empowers people, boosts productivity, and protects the planet.



Our Mision


Our vision is to create a world where everyone has access to clean, affordable, and reliable solar power. We believe that solar power is the key to a sustainable future and we are committed to making it a reality.
